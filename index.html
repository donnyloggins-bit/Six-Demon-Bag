About

No Face.
Only the Sound.

Six Demon Bag has no biography. The figure behind the music wears no name — only a mask, a suit, and a bowler hat. What matters is what comes through the speakers when the lights go out.

The music lives at the intersection of dread and electricity. Blues that crawled up from somewhere below the floorboards. Industrial noise that remembers something you were supposed to forget. A gate swung open onto fire.

All records are self-produced. All releases are anonymous. Every session ends with the tapes sealed and the room locked behind them.
