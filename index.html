About

The Sound
Behind the Noise

Six Demon Bag is a solo project born from the wreckage of late nights and overdriven amplifiers. Rooted in the collision of post-industrial atmospheres and raw blues electricity, the music refuses category and demands attention.

Drawing from a well of influences that spans Nick Cave's gothic narrative, the sonic brutalism of early Nine Inch Nails, and the desolate landscapes of Ennio Morricone, Six Demon Bag carves out a space that feels both ancient and urgently modern.

Every record is recorded in isolation. Every track is a ritual.
